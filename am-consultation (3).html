Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

How quickly will I receive my report?

Standard plans respond within 48 hours, Premium within 24 hours, Elite the same day — often within a few hours of submission.

Can I consult from outside Pakistan?

Yes. We serve patients across Pakistan, the UAE, the UK and internationally. Prescriptions may vary by location — your doctor will advise accordingly.

What happens after my consultation?

All plans include follow-up messages. Premium includes 3, Elite includes unlimited follow-ups for 30 days from the date of your consultation.

Is my information kept private?

Absolutely. All patient data, photos and medical history are handled with strict confidentiality and are never shared with third parties.

What is a video consultation?

Elite patients receive a live video call with their doctor via WhatsApp or Zoom — ideal for complex cases requiring real-time discussion.

How do I pay?

We accept bank transfer, Easypaisa, JazzCash and international card payments. Payment details are provided after you submit your booking request.
